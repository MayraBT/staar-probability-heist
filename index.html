▸ DETECTIVE BRIEFING

At 7:06 AM yesterday, a priceless artifact vanished from the Greiner Vault: The Golden Ratio, a centuries-old gemstone. Five suspects. One culprit. The thief left behind fifteen scenes — each governed by the laws of probability.

Crack each clue. Each correct answer reveals a single character — but be warned: the clues are not in order. Each character must be placed into its assigned slot number on the Evidence Board to spell the final code.

▸ HOW TO CRACK THE CASE:
1. Solve each of the 15 clues below (in any order).
2. Take a photo of your work for each clue before verifying — your work must be visible.
3. Each correct answer auto-fills its character into a numbered slot on the Evidence Board.
4. When all 15 slots are filled, the case will automatically close and reveal the final message.
